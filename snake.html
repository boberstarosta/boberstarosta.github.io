Snake

Instructions

  • Use ↑, ←, ↓, → arrow keys to control the snake.
  • Avoid hitting borders or your own tail.
  • Eat ■ food and your snake will grow.
  • You get score for eating food.
  • Press Pause/Break to pause the game.
